About Me

This is who I am!

Hello! My name is Alex Baker and I am a student here at Brigham Young University and I built this website for my end of semester project for my Information Sciences 201 class. I am currently a sophmore here at BYU and I am taking this class so that I may complete one of the prerequisites to get into the Marriott School of Business.

I was born in Sandy, Utah, to a family of six, where I am the oldest of four kids. We are an exceptionally loud and outgoing bunch who love to eat and go out and explore the world with our dog Kona.

For me, I will always remember the time of COVID-19 fondly, because it was during this time that I met my amazing wife Ash. We met in the first weeks of school in the Fall of 2020 and were shortly married one year later. I am extrememly lucky to have found someone so special in a time that was full of difficulties for many.
